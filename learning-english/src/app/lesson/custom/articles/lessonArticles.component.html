The Indefinite Articles

The indefinite articles "a/an" means "un/una".
If the next word begins with a vowel sound, you use "an" if not you use "a".

Example:

  • A university - Una universidad
  • A hotel - Un hotel
  • An hour - Una hora
  • An ant - Una hormiga
